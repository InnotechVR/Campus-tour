Campus tour in the headset

Live Google Street View panoramas rendered in Three.js. Turn your head to look around, gaze at a marker to move between stops. Runs in the Quest 3 and Pico 4 browsers over WebXR — nothing to install.

You only paste the key once per session per headset. After it loads, press Enter VR (goggles icon, bottom-right) for head-tracked stereo, then gaze at a glowing marker for about a second to walk to the next stop. Edit the STOPS list near the bottom of this file to point it at your own campus (lat/lng & marker headings).
