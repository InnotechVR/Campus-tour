Campus tour in the headset

Head-tracked 360° panoramas — look around by turning your head, tap a marker to move between stops. No API key. Works in the Quest & PICO browsers.

After it loads, press Enter VR (goggles icon, bottom-right) for full head-tracked stereo. To use your campus, shoot equirectangular 360° photos, drop the .jpg files next to this HTML, and set each stop’s image in the STOPS list.
