Campus tour in the headset

Paste a Google Maps API key with the Maps JavaScript API and Street View enabled, then start. The tour loads head-tracked 360° panoramas you look around by turning your head, with tappable points to move between stops.

On the headset: open this page in the Quest Browser or PICO Browser, load the tour, then press Enter VR (bottom-right goggles icon) to go fully immersive and head-tracked. Get a free key at console.cloud.google.com/google/maps-apis — enable “Maps JavaScript API.” Keep this key for testing only; restrict it to your domain before any public use.
